Results

We carried out experiments based on 200 integration scenarios created from a user study and an applet dataset crawled from ifttt.com.

Among all the scenarios we collected, 81.5% of them have been found and confirmed with security risks, and 95.5% of them turn out to have a small attack intensity - the attacker needs to attack only a few nodes to compromise the system. This finding supports our motivation that the security problem is serious in the context of user-customized IFTTT-style HA-IoT systems, and our method can find out the weak points of the system efficiently.

Our experiment is conducted on a workstation with Intel(R) Core(TM) i5-6300HQ CPU @ 2.30GHz, 4GB RAM, Ubuntu 14.04.5 LTS 64-bit.

Table below lists the verification results of the scenarios from the user study and ifttt.com. “U/F/P” (U - unauthorized access, F - financial loss and P - privacy leakage) are the security issue categories.
